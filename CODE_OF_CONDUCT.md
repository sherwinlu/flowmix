# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

FlowMix is a project for people building, testing, and improving audio tooling together. We are committed to making participation in this project welcoming, respectful, and productive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives.
- Give and receive feedback with curiosity and good faith.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the people using it.
- Use welcoming language and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or dismissive behavior.
- Respect privacy and do not share private communications or personal information without consent.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Insults, demeaning comments, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all FlowMix project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and any community channels associated with the project.

## Reporting

To report a code of conduct concern, email **sherwinlu@gmail.com**. Please include relevant context, links, screenshots, or other details that would help the maintainers understand and address the situation.

Security vulnerabilities should follow the separate process in `SECURITY.md` and should not be reported through public GitHub issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they judge appropriate and proportionate, including:

- Asking participants to revise or remove comments.
- Moderating or locking conversations.
- Closing issues or pull requests that violate this code.
- Temporarily or permanently limiting participation in project spaces.

Reports will be reviewed as privately as practical. Participants are expected to cooperate with maintainers when concerns are raised.
